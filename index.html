Design Process

Hi, my name is Charles and I am a product designer. Currently, I am looking for opportunities where I can provide assistance in solving complex problems.

I am a self-taught designer who fell in love with design after traveling and not having a home for the past year. Currently, I am residing in Chicago where I want to continue learning more about the world and to think critically about why things are the way they are. My main motivation is the possibility for change and improvement. What keeps me going is the hope to surrounding myself with intelligent people who can test or validate my assumptions.

What I value about design:
Thoughtful process

Each problem is different, but I believe design deserves a certain cadence. Establishing empathy and an overarching goal at the beginning is invaluable to me. Setting these guidelines early on constantly reminds me of who I am solving these problems for. It keeps me humble and nimble in the sense that my designs are not about me or my successes. It is the problems that we are solving that continue to motivate me. The ability to continuously iterate and seek out better solutions is also great.


Holistic storytelling

What is the story the product is trying to encourage? I believe the center of design is the ability to tell a great story. Storytelling within technology especially has this great ability to reach more people. And each story is different. Design can be seen as a very esthetic discipline, but I am more interested in how visual design can influence the way we feel about things.
