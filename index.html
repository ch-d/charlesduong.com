VALUES
Thoughtful Process

Each problem is different, but I believe design deserves a certain cadence. Establishing empathy and an overarching goal at the beginning is invaluable to me. Setting guidelines early on constantly reminds me who I am solving problems for. It keeps me humble in the sense that my designs are not about me or my successes; the user and their needs are my main motivation.

Modular Thinking

Coming from an engineering background, I believe in creating tools and systems to reduce redundant tasks when designing. Having modular design systems in mind helps me push out pixels and ideas faster. This in turn creates a smoother *interaction?* when ideating and iterating concepts. Great design can only come from testing and prototyping out all the possibilities.

Holistic Storytelling

What is the story the product is trying to encourage? What is the hero of the design? What is the context the user will be in? Through asking all the right questions, you have the ability to understanding all the constraints. Having the ability to sift through all the noise and prune it down to the core essence is something I wish to continuously improve upon. I believe designs can only come from having an ability to communicate its value.
